Development Journey
My fascination with development sparked 4 years ago, diving straight into code using Python, which is the ultimate baseline foundation for system logic. It opened my eyes to algorithmic architecture and structured problem-solving paradigms.


A major roadblock surfaced early on: my school lacked any form of Computer Science curriculum. Without structured engineering classes or local guidance, I had to completely shift gears toward self-learning, researching technical documentation entirely on my own.


Everything clicked when I discovered web ecosystem engineering. Navigating through standard styling mechanisms and complex DOM logic made me realize my ultimate purpose: engineering scalable, useful interfaces that provide global utility to real people.
